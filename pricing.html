// FAQ

Pricing & billing questions

Do I pay for LLM tokens on top?+

Yes. LLM and AWS infrastructure costs go directly on your AWS / Anthropic bills — we do not resell them. For typical Starter usage this is $50–$150 / month.

Can I self-host?+

Vega always deploys into your AWS account. We ship a Terraform module and a signed container image; the control plane is the only thing that runs in our account, and you can audit every call.

Is there a free trial?+

The private beta (Q4 2026) is free for the first three months for design partners who sign a light LOI. Apply here.

How are “runs” counted?+

A run is one complete execution of an agent from trigger to final outcome, including any retries. Internal steps, human-in-the-loop approvals, and connector calls do not count separately.

Can I switch plans?+

Any time, pro-rated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Startup discount?+

Yes — 50% off for 12 months for any company ≤ 5 people in the first year of operation. Just mention it in the contact form.
