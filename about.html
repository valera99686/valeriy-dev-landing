// Mission

Make automation the default, not the exception.

Every year, B2B teams burn an enormous amount of human attention on work that is repetitive, rule-driven, and invisible to the customer — triaging inboxes, reconciling data, copying numbers between systems, answering the same questions on Telegram.

We believe the combination of modern LLMs, a rigorous engineering culture, and a small surface area of well-chosen cloud primitives makes it possible, for the first time, to replace that work safely and affordably. Our job is to turn that belief into shipped product.

// Principles

How we build

Ship small, ship often

Weekly production deploys, feature flags, reversible migrations. A customer never waits three months to see their first result.

Own the full stack

The same engineer writes the Python agent, the Terraform, and the Telegram bot. Fewer hand-offs, faster learning loops, no bus-factor-of-one.

Boring technology for the core

Postgres, S3, Lambda, FastAPI. We save our novelty budget for the AI layer, where it actually changes what the product can do.

Privacy by default

Isolated AWS accounts per client, EU data residency by default, mutual NDAs before any technical discussion.
